# Logo Update for KrishiMitra

## Overview
The KrishiMitra logo has been updated with a new design that better represents the agricultural focus of the application.

## Previous Logo
The previous logo featured:
- A farmer silhouette with a hat
- A tractor
- Decorative leaves and a water droplet
- A curved vine design

## New Logo Design
The updated logo features:
- A vibrant sun in the background
- A wheat field with waving wheat stalks
- A farmer character holding farming tools
- The "KrishiMitra" text at the bottom
- Earthy colors that represent agriculture and nature

## Design Elements
1. **Sun**: Represents energy and growth
2. **Wheat Field**: Symbolizes agriculture and farming
3. **Farmer Character**: Shows the human element in farming
4. **Tools**: Represents the practical aspects of farming
5. **Color Scheme**: 
   - Green shades for the wheat field (growth and nature)
   - Yellow/orange for the sun (energy and warmth)
   - Blue for the farmer's clothing (trust and reliability)

## Implementation
The logo file `krishimitra-logo.svg` has been updated in the public directory. The logo is used in:
1. The login page (in two locations)
2. Any other pages that reference `/krishimitra-logo.svg`

## Verification
The application builds successfully with the new logo, confirming that the update was implemented correctly.

## Benefits
- More clearly represents the agricultural focus of the application
- Uses colors that are associated with farming and nature
- Simpler design that is easily recognizable
- Maintains the brand identity while improving visual appeal
